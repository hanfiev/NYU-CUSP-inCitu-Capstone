Halletts North
Brief
A private application by Astoria Owners LLC to pursue a ZM from M1-1 to R7-3/C2-4, a ZR to Appendix F to map an MIH area, a ZS pursuant to ZR 62-837(a) to waive height, setback, and tower regulations, a ZA to modify the WPAA and allow for phased development of the open space, a ZC pursuant to ZR 62-81, and an amendment to the City Map to demap a portion of 3rd St to facilitate a 987,000sf mixed use development with 1,400 units and 42,208sf of open space at 3-15 26th Ave in Astoria, CD 1, Queens.
